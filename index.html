04

The solution

A mat that
guides you.

ALIGNA is not a yoga mat. It is a guided movement system — designed around the exercises that actually relieve lower back pain, with precision markings printed directly into the surface.

Center alignment rings

Concentric guides help you center your spine and pelvis with every single exercise — no guesswork, no drift.

Angle reference lines

45° and 90° guides ensure your limbs and joints move through correct, safe ranges of motion.

Body zone markers

Defined shoulder, hip, knee, and foot placement zones for every position in your routine.

The founder
"I was diagnosed with a herniated disc. The exercises existed — I just needed a way to do them correctly, every day, at home."

Five years ago, I faced a diagnosis that changed how I thought about movement. After months of physical therapy, I developed a personal routine — but I kept losing alignment without visual reference.

I designed ALIGNA for myself first. A mat that made every exercise unambiguous. Where to place my hands. Where my hips should be. How far my knees should travel. After years of daily use and refinement, I decided to share it.
