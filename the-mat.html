What makes it different

Every mark has
a purpose.

01

Center alignment system

Concentric rings and a precision center point let you instantly locate your spine's neutral position. Every exercise starts from a known reference — not a guess.

02

Angle reference guides

45° and 90° angle lines ensure your limbs travel through safe, correct arcs of motion. Eliminate the risk of overextension or poor hip rotation during your routine.

03

Body zone markers

Shoulder, hip, knee, and foot placement zones are clearly defined for every key exercise. No more wondering if you're in the right starting position.

04

Consistency ruler marks

Lateral ruler-style marks along the edges help you reproduce exercises identically session after session — essential for progress and injury prevention.

Questions

Frequently asked

Is ALIGNA a yoga mat?+
No. While ALIGNA can be used for yoga, it was specifically designed for guided movement routines — particularly exercises that support lower back health and alignment. The markings are calibrated for this purpose, not for general yoga practice.
Is it suitable for complete beginners?+
Absolutely. The alignment markings were designed precisely for beginners who need visual reference. You don't need prior experience — the mat guides you through correct positioning from your very first session.
Is this a medical product?+
No. ALIGNA is a wellness and movement product. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. We always recommend consulting a qualified healthcare professional before beginning any new exercise program, especially if you have a diagnosed condition.
How thick is the mat?+
ALIGNA is designed with a 6mm premium density layer — thick enough for joint cushioning during floor exercises, firm enough to maintain stability during movement. Full specifications will be published at launch.
How do I clean it?+
ALIGNA's surface is easy to maintain. Wipe down with a damp cloth and mild soap after use. The alignment markings are printed into the material — not surface-applied — so they will not fade or wear with regular cleaning.
